Understand Conditions

Browse through our comprehensive guide of common ailments to understand symptoms and professional care options.

Chronic

Diabetes Mellitus

Issues managing blood glucose levels effectively.

  • Increased Thirst
  • Fatigue
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Monitor glucose levels daily and maintain a low-glycemic diet.

Chronic

Thyroid Disorder

Hormonal imbalance affecting metabolism and energy.

  • Weight Changes
  • Mood Swings
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Take prescribed medication 30 mins before breakfast for best absorption.

Respiratory

Asthma

Inflammation of airways causing breathing difficulties.

  • Wheezing
  • Chest Tightness
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Always carry a rescue inhaler and identify environmental triggers.

Respiratory

COPD

Chronic obstructive lung disease affecting long-term airflow.

  • Chronic Cough
  • Shortness of Breath
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Avoid smoke exposure and practice pulmonary rehabilitation exercises.

Neurological

Migraine

Severe recurring headaches with sensory sensitivity.

  • Pulsing Pain
  • Nausea
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Rest in a dark room and maintain a consistent sleep-wake cycle.

Neurological

Epilepsy

Nervous system disorder characterized by seizures.

  • Muscle Spasms
  • Loss of Awareness
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Wear a medical alert bracelet and ensure adequate night-time rest.

Lifestyle

Hypertension

Chronic high blood pressure affecting arterial health.

  • Headaches
  • Dizziness
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Reduce salt intake and engage in 30 mins of daily cardio.

Lifestyle

Insomnia

Persistent difficulty in falling or staying asleep.

  • Daytime Fatigue
  • Poor Focus
Healthcare+ Suggestion:

Limit caffeine after 2 PM and avoid screens 1 hour before bed.
