Breath-Tech

Innovation in Thoracic Medicine

Ex-Vivo Lung Perfusion (EVLP)

A pioneering technique where lungs are "reconditioned" outside the body. This allows us to improve the health of donor lungs that were previously considered unusable.

  • Increases Donor Pool
  • Real-time Graft Monitoring

Digital Lung Bio-Analytics

Using AI to track lung mechanics and oxygen saturation 24/7, providing early warning signals for graft rejection or infection.

Electromagnetic Navigation Bronchoscopy

GPS-like technology for the lungs that allows surgeons to reach deep lesions for biopsy and treatment without invasive incisions.
