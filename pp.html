Privacy Policy

At HealthCare+, we understand that your health information is deeply personal. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle the data you entrust to us.

Our Privacy Promise:
  • We never sell your medical records to third parties.
  • All data is encrypted using 256-bit SSL protocols.
  • You have the right to request data deletion at any time.

1. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality medical services, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identity: Name, date of birth, and government-issued ID (when required).
  • Contact Details: Email address, phone number, and physical address for medicine delivery.
  • Medical History: Past diagnoses, medications, lab results, and consultation notes.
  • Technical Data: IP address and browser type to improve our app performance.

2. How We Protect Your Data

Your data is stored on secure servers located in Tier-4 data centers. We implement strict access controls, meaning only your assigned doctor and authorized medical staff can view your health records. In the event of a data breach, we are legally bound to notify you within 72 hours.

3. Data Sharing

We only share your information with third parties when necessary for your care:

  • Pharmacies: To fulfill your prescriptions.
  • Insurance Providers: To process claims and verify coverage.
  • Laboratories: To process your test samples and return results.

4. Your Legal Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access and download your entire medical history from our platform.
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your personal or medical information.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (this may limit our ability to provide certain services).
