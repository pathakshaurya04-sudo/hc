Neuro Infrastructure

Tomorrow's Technology Today

Intraoperative MRI (iMRI)

The Brain Suite allows surgeons to take high-resolution MRIs *during* surgery to ensure 100% of a tumor is removed before the patient leaves the OR.

  • Real-time Brain Mapping
  • Reduced Re-operation Rates

CyberKnife S7 System

Robotic radiosurgery that delivers high-dose radiation to tumors anywhere in the brain and spine with sub-millimeter accuracy, without any incisions.

  • Non-Invasive Brain Surgery
  • No Hospital Stay Required

3D Neuro-Navigation

Like GPS for the brain, this system provides a 3D visual guide during surgery, allowing for pinpoint accuracy in the most delicate areas of the nervous system.

  • Enhanced Patient Safety
  • Minimally Invasive Access
