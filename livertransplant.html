Clinical Expertise

Diseases We Treat

01. Living Donor (LDLT)

Advanced surgery allowing a healthy relative to donate a portion of their liver.

02. Pediatric Transplant

Life-saving care for infants with Biliary Atresia and metabolic disorders.

03. Split Liver Surgery

Using one deceased donor liver to save two separate recipients (Adult + Child).

04. ABO Incompatible

Specialized protocols for transplants where donor/recipient blood groups don't match.

05. Liver Cancer (HCC)

Early-stage Hepatocellular Carcinoma management through curative transplant.

06. NASH & Fatty Liver

Treating end-stage cirrhosis caused by Non-Alcoholic Steatohepatitis.

07. Metabolic Disorders

Correction of genetic liver diseases like Wilson's and Urea Cycle disorders.

The Edge

5 Pillars of Technology

01. Machine Perfusion

"Organ-care" tech that keeps livers functioning outside the body before transplant.

02. Da Vinci Robotics

Robot-assisted donor surgery for minimal pain and ultra-fast recovery times.

03. AI 3D Volumetry

Precise 3D reconstruction of liver anatomy to ensure perfect donor-recipient matching.

04. MARS Therapy

Molecular Adsorbent System for artificial liver support in acute failure cases.

05. Flow Cytometry

Real-time immune monitoring to prevent rejection and manage drug levels.
