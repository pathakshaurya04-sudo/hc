Future Tech

Precision Robotics & Innovation

Mako SmartRobotics™

A highly advanced robotic-arm assisted system that allows surgeons to create a personalized surgical plan based on a 3D CT scan of the patient's unique anatomy.

  • AccuStop™ Haptic Technology
  • Bone-Preserving Accuracy

3D Printed Implants

We utilize patient-specific 3D printing technology to create custom titanium implants that match the exact shape of your bone structure for a perfect fit.

  • Customized Anatomical Fit
  • Improved Long-term Outcomes

Mixed Reality Navigation

Surgeons use AR headsets to overlay digital 3D models of the patient's internal anatomy directly onto the surgical field, providing X-ray vision in real-time.

  • Real-time Holographic Guidance
  • Enhanced Spatial Awareness

IoT Recovery Tracking

Post-surgery, patients are equipped with smart knee/hip braces that sync range-of-motion data to our doctors, allowing for remote physical therapy adjustments.

  • 24/7 Progress Monitoring
  • Personalized Exercise Feedback

Bio-Integrative Scaffolding

We utilize advanced biological mesh and scaffolds that encourage natural bone and tissue regrowth, eventually dissolving once the body has fully healed.

  • Bio-Resorbable Materials
  • Minimal Long-term Foreign Bodies
