Clinical Specialties

Clinical Expertise

Leading Cardiac Treatments

TAVI (TAVR)

TAVI is a surgical procedure performed to replace a blocked or narrowed aortic valve in people who are at greater risk or too sick for open heart surgery.

Coronary Artery Bypass (CABG)

A critical surgical procedure (CABG) used to treat coronary artery disease by redirecting blood flow around section of a blocked artery.

Peripheral Angioplasty

Utilizing minimally invasive techniques to open narrowed or blocked blood vessels that supply the legs and other peripheral organs.

Aneurysm Repair

Specialized surgical and endovascular repairs to treat bulging or weakened areas in the blood vessels, ensuring structural integrity.

Clinical Scope

Comprehensive Heart Conditions

Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)

The narrowing of the coronary arteries usually caused by plaque buildup. Our center provides specialized intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) to map blockages with micron-level accuracy.

Stable Angina Heart Attack Prevention

Valvular Heart Disease

Damage or defect in one of the four heart valves. We specialize in TAVI and minimally invasive valve repair.

Arrhythmia & AFib

Irregular heart rhythms treated using advanced electrophysiology and radiofrequency ablation.

Congenital Heart Defects

Structural problems present from birth, managed by our specialized pediatric cardiac surgical wing.

Advanced Heart Failure

Chronic condition where the heart doesn't pump blood as well as it should, requiring VAD or transplant evaluation.

Aortic Aneurysm

A dangerous bulge in the wall of the aorta, treated via high-precision endovascular stent-grafting.

Cardiomyopathy

Genetic or acquired diseases of the heart muscle that can lead to heart failure or systemic complications.

Advanced Technology

Artis-Zeego Cath Lab

HealthCare+ is equipped with the Artis-Zeego robotic imaging system. This technology provides unparalleled 3D road-mapping, allowing our surgeons to navigate complex vascular structures with laser-like precision.

  • Robotic-assisted Angiography
  • Real-time 3D Reconstruction

AI-Driven Cardiac MRI

We utilize advanced Artificial Intelligence algorithms to analyze cardiac scans. This allows for early detection of heart failure and valve issues that might be invisible to the naked eye.

  • Predictive Plaque Analysis
  • Automated Ejection Fraction Calculation

Modular Hybrid OT

Our Hybrid Operating Theatres combine high-end surgical facilities with advanced imaging. This enables doctors to switch from minimally invasive to open-heart surgery instantly if needed.
