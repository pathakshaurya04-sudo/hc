Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our medical services, digital consultations, and patient care.

How do I book a video consultation?

You can book a video consultation by clicking the "Book Appointment" button. Select "Online Consultation" as your visit type, choose your preferred doctor and time slot, and complete the secure payment process.

Do you accept major health insurance?

Yes, HealthCare+ partners with most major insurance providers. During registration, you can upload your insurance details, and our billing team will verify your coverage automatically.

Can I get my prescriptions delivered?

Absolutely. After your consultation, digital prescriptions are sent to our integrated pharmacy. You can opt for home delivery through our "Store" section, usually arriving within 2-4 hours in Lucknow.

How do I access my lab test results?

All laboratory and imaging results are uploaded directly to your "Health Library" portal. You will receive an SMS and email notification as soon as your reports are ready for download.

What is your policy on emergency care?

For life-threatening emergencies, please call our 24/7 hotline at +91 522 123 4567 immediately. We have a dedicated ambulance fleet and a Level 1 Trauma Center ready for urgent admissions.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with your needs.
